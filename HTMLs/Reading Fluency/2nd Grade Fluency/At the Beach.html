At the Beach Ben and his family walked on the beach. Ben liked the beach and the sunshine. He hopped in the sand. It was rough with pebbles, but he kept hopping. Ben spotted fish in a narrow bit of water. They were swimming close together. His father snapped a photo. Then Ben watched two crabs hiding near some shells. He wondered which shell the bigger crab would decide to live in. He also saw a few birds flying high in the sky. Would they catch some fish to eat? Many people were swimming and splashing in the water. A dog chased a yellow ball. Ben saw children making sandcastles. What would it be like to join them later? They were having a lot of fun. He sat near some children on the beach and watched. Then he helped to make the sandcastle. Ben had a happy day at the beach!
