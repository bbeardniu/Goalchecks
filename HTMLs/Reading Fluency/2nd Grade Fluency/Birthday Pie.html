Birthday Pie Jason is glad today. It is his birthday. Five friends will come to his house to have a picnic lunch and eat snacks. Next the children will tell jokes and play games. Jason will rip open his gifts. Will his friends bring a tent, a bug box, or a frog kit? Those were some gifts on his list. He will soon see what his friends will bring. Will they eat birthday cake? No! Jason does not like birthday cake. Cake is too sweet. He does not like nuts or jam or plums, either. Jason likes pie. He helps his mom sift flour until she digs in to make the crust. He licks his lips. This pie will be the best. His friends will love it. Jason’s mother will stick candles on his pie. She will turn off the lights. Then his friends and family will sit around to sing. “Happy Birthday to you!” The song will echo through the house.
